Who am I?

Luis Angel Trujillo Garcia, Computer System Engineer.

I am a handsome guy who enjoy solving programming problems, the languages that I know are: Java, C++, MySQL; actually I interested in Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, in this page you could see some of my most recently projects.

My participation in Code Jam

Soft Skill

The contest required know difficult topics that I have learned by myself, so I am an autodidact person who is interested in learn more.
